We open registrations on January every year. Our courses are hotcake and typically gets its limit within the first day of opening so if you really want to enroll, then get prepared to enroll on the first day.

This course is for anyone wanting to transition into tech and improve their learning journey. This course will give you all you need to become the better developer you dream of

All our courses are moderated by certified profesors. Our moderators are people with a success track record. Our of the 1 million people who register for our courses every year, 990, 000 end up getting jobs within one month after the course completion

Listowel Adolwin is our founder. He founded this organization in 2015 when he was in high school. At the time, it was a small project he thought would help his colleaque students to learn and pass their exams. After realizing how helpful it was to his mates in high school, he decided to widen that help to everyone single person in the world.

This is a completely free course for everyone. Ever since the start of this program, it is always been free for all. However, there has been many instances where past benefiaries of the program decide to donate to help program grow and continue to impact more lives

The course duration varies from course to course. But they typically take 10 weeks to complete except a few of then that can take as long as 10 weeks
